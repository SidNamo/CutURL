Privacy Policy

This Chrome extension, Cut URL, does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

The extension only accesses the current tab's URL upon user interaction (clicking the extension icon), and sends that URL to a backend server for shortening.

No names, email addresses, IP addresses, or any identifying information is collected or retained.
